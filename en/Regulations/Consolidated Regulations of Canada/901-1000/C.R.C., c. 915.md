# By-Laws Respecting the Tariff of Small Vessel Facilities for the Port Alberni Harbour Commission (British Columbia)

**C.R.C., c. 915**

Enabling authorities: 
- [HARBOUR COMMISSIONS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/H/H-1.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This By-law may be cited as the Port Alberni Harbour Small Vessel Facilities Tariff By-law.
> SOR/78-350, s. 1(F); SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).





## Interpretation
> [SOR/94-697, s. 2(F)]



**2** In this By-law,

***Act*** means the [Harbour Commissions Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/H/H-1.md); (*Loi*)

***attendant*** means a person appointed by the Commission to have charge of small vessel facilities and to collect rates in accordance with this By-law; (*préposé*)

***berthage*** means a rate imposed on a vessel in respect of the period of time that the vessel is
- **(a)** moored to a small vessel facility,
- **(b)** occupying a berth or any space at or near a small vessel facility, or
- **(c)** secured in any manner to a vessel that is described in paragraph (a) or (b); (*droit d’amarrage à quai*)

***Commission*** means the Port Alberni Harbour Commission; (*Commission*)

***goods*** means any movables other than vessels; (*marchandises*)

***month*** means the period commencing on a date in one calendar month and terminating on the day immediately preceding the same date in the next calendar month, or, if there is no corresponding date in the next calendar month, terminating on the last day of that calendar month; (*mois*)

***owner*** includes
- **(a)** in respect of a vessel, the agent, charterer by demise or master of the vessel, and
- **(b)** in respect of goods, the agent, sender, consignee or bailee of the goods as well as the carrier of the goods to, upon, over or from a small vessel facility; (*propriétaire*)

***small vessel facility*** means wharves, floats, ramps, utilities and other services referred to in the schedule, administered by the Commission for the accommodation of small vessels; (*installation pour petits bâtiments*)

***wharfage*** means a rate imposed on goods
- **(a)** that are loaded on or unloaded from a vessel at a small vessel facility, or
- **(b)** that are placed on, conveyed across, along, over or under a small vessel facility. (*quayage*)
> SOR/79-614, s. 1; SOR/81-62, s. 1; SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).





## Powers of the Commission


**3** The Commission, in order to accommodate a commercial fishing vessel or other commercial vessel, may at any time
- **(a)** refuse to accommodate a non-commercial pleasure vessel; or
- **(b)** require a non-commercial pleasure vessel to leave its position at a small vessel facility.
> SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).





## Rates


**4** The rates set out in the schedule are hereby imposed for the services specified in the schedule.



**5** 

- **(1)** Subject to subsection (2), berthage is payable in respect of a vessel on the overall length of the vessel for the period during which it is berthed.

- **(2)** Berthage is not payable on a Canadian commercial fishing vessel that is employed exclusively in fishing during the period from May 1 to October 31 in any calendar year for the first seven days after berthing where it uses a small vessel facility for
	- **(a)** landing the vessel’s catch,
	- **(b)** obtaining supplies, or
	- **(c)** making repairs
following any period of four or more days at sea.
> SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).




**6** 

- **(1)** Subject to subsection (2), the rates imposed by this By-law in respect of a vessel are payable by the owner of the vessel to the Commission when they are incurred.

- **(2)** [Revoked, SOR/78-303, Sch. III, s. 2]
> SOR/78-303, Sch. III, s. 2; SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).




**7** Where the rates on a vessel have been paid in advance and the Commission requires the vessel to vacate its position at a small vessel facility, a pro rata refund of the rates paid shall be made in respect of the unused period of berthage.
> SOR/78-350, s. 1; SOR/79-614, s. 2; SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).




**8** The rates imposed by this By-law are in addition to any other rates payable to the Commission that are imposed by any other by-law made under the Act.
> SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).





## Overall Length of Vessel


**9** The overall length of a vessel may be determined by the attendant for the purpose of this By-law.
> SOR/94-697, s. 2(F).
