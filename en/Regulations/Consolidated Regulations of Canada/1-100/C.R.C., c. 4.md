# Regulations Respecting Air Cushion Vehicles

**C.R.C., c. 4**

Enabling authorities: 
- [AERONAUTICS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/A/A-2.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Air Cushion Vehicle Regulations.




## Interpretation


**2** In these Regulations, ***air cushion vehicle*** means a machine designed to derive support in the atmosphere primarily from reactions against the earth’s surface of air expelled from the machine.




## Exclusion


**3** Commercial air services provided by means of air cushion vehicles are excluded from the operation of Part II of the [Aeronautics Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/A/A-2.md).
