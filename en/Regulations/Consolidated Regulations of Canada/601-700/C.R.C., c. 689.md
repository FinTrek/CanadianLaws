# Order Authorizing the Loan of Defence Materiel and Equipment to Contractors

**C.R.C., c. 689**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the Defence Materiel and Equipment Loaned to Contractors Order.




## Authorization


**2** The Minister of National Defence is authorized to lend, on such terms and conditions as he may deem appropriate and in the interests of defence,
- **(a)** materiel, other than prototypes, sealed samples, models and catering contractor’s equipment, to the Department of Supply and Services for the use of its contractors; and
- **(b)** prototypes, sealed samples, models and catering contractor’s equipment directly to contractors of the Department of Supply and Services engaged in work for national defence.
