# Game Declared in Danger of Becoming Extinct

**C.R.C., c. 1610**

Enabling authorities: 
- [YUKON ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%207.md)

----------



## Declaration


**1** The game listed in the schedule is hereby declared to be game in danger of becoming extinct pursuant to subsection 17(3) of the [Yukon Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%207.md).




### **SCHEDULE** 
- Bison (buffalo)
- Black-tailed deer
- Elk
- Musk-oxen
