# Order Designating Certain Portions of the Federal Public Administration

**C.R.C., c. 1336**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

----------


**1** [Repealed, SI/2015-48, s. 2]




## Designation


**2** The portions of the federal public administration listed in the schedule are designated as parts of the public service, and the employees in those portions of the federal public administration who are not otherwise employed in the public service are deemed, for the purposes of subsection 35(2) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md), to be employed in the public service.
> SI/2015-48, s. 3.





### **SCHEDULE** 
**(s. 2)**
- Atomic Energy of Canada Limited
- Canada Council for the Arts
- Canadian Commercial Corporation
- Canadian Council of Ministers of the Environment
- Canadian Tourism Commission
- Defence Construction (1951) Limited
- Export Development Canada
- Farm Credit Canada
- Government of Northwest Territories
- Government of Nunavut
- Government of the Yukon Territory
- National Arts Centre Corporation
- National Battlefields Commission
- Royal Canadian Mint
- Seaway International Bridge Corporation Limited
> SI/78-61, s. 1; SI/78-76, s. 1; SI/89-178, s. 1; 2001, c. 22, s. 22, c. 33, s. 30, c. 34, s. 17; SI/2001-118, s. 1; SI/2015-48, ss. 4, 5.
