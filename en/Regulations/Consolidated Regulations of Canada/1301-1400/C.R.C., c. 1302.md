# Order Designating the Minister for the Purposes of the Precious Metals Marking Act

**C.R.C., c. 1302**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PRECIOUS METALS MARKING ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-19.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the Minister Designation Order (Precious Metals Marking Act).




## Designation


**2** The Minister of Consumer and Corporate Affairs, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, is hereby designated as the Minister for the purposes of the [Precious Metals Marking Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-19.md).
