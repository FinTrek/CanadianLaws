# Order Approving the Exclusion by the Public Service Commission from the Operation of the Public Service Employment Act of Positions and Employees of the Communications Security Establishment

**C.R.C., c. 1342**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the Communications Security Establishment Exclusion of Positions and Employees Approval Order.




## Approval


**2** The exclusion, by the Public Service Commission, from the operation of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md), other than section 35 thereof, effective April 1, 1975, of all positions and employees of the Communications Security Establishment of the Department of National Defence, is hereby approved.
