# Order Declaring that Grosse Isle, P.Q., Is a Prohibited Place

**C.R.C., c. 1245**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SECURITY OF INFORMATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/O/O-5.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as Grosse Isle, P.Q., Prohibited Place Order.




## Declaration


**2** Grosse Isle, County of Montmagny, Province of Quebec is hereby declared to be a prohibited place within the meaning of the [Security of Information Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/O/O-5.md). 
> 2001, c. 41, s. 39.





## Application


**3** This Order does not apply to over-flying aircraft.
