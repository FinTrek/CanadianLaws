# Game Declared in Danger of Becoming Extinct

**C.R.C., c. 1236**

Enabling authorities: 
- [NORTHWEST TERRITORIES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2014/c.%202,%20s.%202.md)

----------


**1** The Governor in Council hereby declares the game listed in the schedule as game in danger of becoming extinct.




### **SCHEDULE** 
- Barren-ground caribou
- Musk-ox
- Polar bear
- Wood buffalo
