# Order Exempting the C.N.R. Company from Subsection 10(2) of the Official Languages Act

**C.R.C., c. 1244**

Enabling authorities: 
- [OFFICIAL LANGUAGES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2031%20(4th%20Supp.).md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the C.N.R. Company Exemption Order.




## Exemption


**2** The Canadian National Railway Company is hereby exempted from the application of subsection 10(2) of the [Official Languages Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2031%20(4th%20Supp.).md) in respect of services provided or made available by it to the public, other than to the travelling public, at places elsewhere than in Canada.
