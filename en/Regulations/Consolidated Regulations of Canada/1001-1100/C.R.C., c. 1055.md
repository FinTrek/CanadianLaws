# Order Respecting the Application of Part IV of the National Energy Board Act

**C.R.C., c. 1055**

Enabling authorities: 
- [NATIONAL ENERGY BOARD ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/N/N-7.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the National Energy Board Order No. MO-62-69.




## Application of part IV of the act


**2** The National Energy Board, pursuant to subsection 97(1)<sup><a href='#footnote1_e'>[1]</a></sup> of the [National Energy Board Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/N/N-7.md), hereby orders that Part IV of the said Act applies to every person who was operating a pipeline on November 2, 1959.

<a name='footnote1_e'><sup>1</sup></a>: This subsection is not reproduced in the R.S.C., 1970, see S.C., 1959, c. 46.<br />
