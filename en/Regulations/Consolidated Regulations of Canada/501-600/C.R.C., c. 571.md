# Regulations Prescribing Fees for Licences Issued Under the Excise Act

**C.R.C., c. 571**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXCISE ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-14.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Excise Act Licence Fees Regulations.




## Fees


**2** Every person to whom a licence is granted under the [Excise Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-14.md) for a purpose set out in Column I of an item of the schedule shall pay the annual fee set out in Column II of that item.
