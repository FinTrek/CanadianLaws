# Order Pursuant to Section 13 of the Financial Administration Act Authorizing the Minister of Agriculture to Prescribe Certain Fees

**C.R.C., c. 712**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the Minister of Agriculture Authority to Prescribe Fees Order.




## Authorization


**2** The Minister of Agriculture is authorized to prescribe by order the fees to be charged for services provided by Her Majesty with respect to
- **(a)** the inspection of milk production of dairy cattle and dairy goats in Canada; and
- **(b)** the performance testing of beef cattle, sheep and swine in Canada.
> SI/85-125, s. 1.
