# Order Authorizing the Minister of National Defence to Transfer Academic Hoods to Recipients of Honorary Degrees

**C.R.C., c. 736**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

----------



## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the Transfer of Royal Military College Academic Hoods Order.




## Authorization


**2** The Minister of National Defence is authorized to transfer academic hoods from time to time to recipients of honorary degrees conferred by the Royal Military College of Canada, Kingston, Ontario.
