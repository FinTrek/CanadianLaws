# Designating the Civil Aviation Tribunal as a Department; the Minister of Transport as Appropriate Minister for Purposes of the Financial Administration Act and as Minister for Purposes of Section 31 of the Aeronautics Act

**SI/86-64**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

- [AERONAUTICS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/A/A-2.md)

Date registered: 14 May 1986

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, is pleased hereby

**(a)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition of ***department*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), to designate the Civil Aviation Tribunal as a department for the purposes of that Act;

**(b)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition of ***appropriate Minister*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), to designate the Minister of Transport as the appropriate Minister with respect to the Civil Aviation Tribunal for the purposes of that Act; and

**(c)** pursuant to section 31<sup><a href='#footnote1star_e'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Aeronautics Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/A/A-2.md), to designate the Minister of Transport, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the Minister for the purposes of that section.

<a name='footnote1star_e'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1985, c. 28, s. 5<br />
