# Order Designating the Registrar of the Competition Tribunal as Deputy Head with Respect to the Registry of the Competition Tribunal

**SI/86-216**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 24 December 1986

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, is pleased hereby, pursuant to the definition ***deputy head*** in subsection 2(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md), to designate for the purposes of that Act the Registrar of the Competition Tribunal as the deputy head of that portion of the Public Service of Canada appointed under subsection 14(2) of the [Competition Tribunal Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2019%20(2nd%20Supp.).md).
