# Order transferring from the Prime Minister to the President of the Treasury Board the control and supervision of The Leadership Network

**SI/2001-51**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 25 April 2001

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), hereby transfers from the Prime Minister to the President of the Treasury Board the control and supervision of that portion of the public service known as The Leadership Network.
