# Order Directing that Certain Documents be Discontinued

**SI/99-130**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 10 November 1999

----------

Whereas it appears to the Governor in Council that the documents set out in the annexed schedule, each required by an Act of Parliament to be laid before one or both Houses of Parliament, contain the same information as or less information than is contained in the Public Accounts or in any estimates of expenditures submitted to Parliament;

Therefore, Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance and the Treasury Board, pursuant to section 157<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), hereby directs that the documents set out in the annexed schedule be discontinued and hereafter they need not be prepared or laid before either House of Parliament.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 24, s. 47<br />




### **SCHEDULE** 
**1** Annual Report on the Administration of the [Canada Student Loans Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-23.md) ([Canada Student Loans Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-23.md), s. 22)


**2** Annual Report on the Administration of the [Old Age Security Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/O/O-9.md) ([Old Age Security Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/O/O-9.md), s. 47)


**3** Annual Report on business done under the [Government Annuities Improvement Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1974-75-76/c.%2083.md) ([Government Annuities Improvement Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1974-75-76/c.%2083.md), s. 18)


**4** Annual Report on business done under the [Civil Service Insurance Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1952/c.%2049.md) ([Civil Service Insurance Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1952/c.%2049.md), s. 21)


**5** Annual Report on the Administration of the Farm Improvement and Marketing Cooperatives Loans Act and the [Farm Improvement Loans Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-3.md) (Farm Improvement and Marketing Cooperatives Loans Act, s. 22)


**6** Annual Report of the National Farm Products Council ([Farm Products Agencies Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-4.md), s. 15)


**7** Annual Report of the Canadian Grain Commission ([Canada Grain Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/G/G-10.md), s. 15)


**8** Annual Report on the operation of agreements under the [Farm Income Protection Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2022.md) ([Farm Income Protection Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2022.md), s. 21)


**9** Annual Report on the administration of the [Agricultural Marketing Programs Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2020.md) ([Agricultural Marketing Programs Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2020.md), s. 41)


**10** Annual Report on the activities of the Immigration and Refugee Board (Immigration Act, s. 66)


**11** Annual Report on the proceedings under the [Library and Archives of Canada Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2004/c.%2011.md) ([Library and Archives of Canada Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2004/c.%2011.md), s. 15)


> 2004, c.11, 52.
