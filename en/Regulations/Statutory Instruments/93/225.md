# Order Designating the Commission of Inquiry on the Blood System in Canada as a Department and the Prime Minister as Appropriate Minister

**SI/93-225**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 15 December 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General In Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, is pleased hereby

**(a)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition ***department*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), to designate the Commission of Inquiry on the Blood System in Canada as a department for the purposes of that Act; and



**(b)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition ***appropriate Minister*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), to designate the Prime Minister as the appropriate Minister with respect to the Commission of Inquiry on the Blood System in Canada.
