# Reservation to the Crown Waiver Order (Prosperous Lake, N.T.)

**SI/93-240**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TERRITORIAL LANDS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/T/T-7.md)

Date registered: 29 December 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to paragraph 13(b) of the [Territorial Lands Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/T/T-7.md), is pleased hereby to order that any portion of the lands described in the schedule hereto lying within 30.48 metres (100 feet) of the Ordinary High Water mark of Prosperous Lake in the Northwest Territories be included in the sale of the said lands.




### **SCHEDULE** 
The whole of Lots numbered 1088 to 1115 inclusive in Quad numbered 85 J/9, Prosperous Lake, in the Northwest Territories, as said lots are shown on a plan of survey of record number 75196 in the Canada Lands Surveys Records at Ottawa, a copy of which is filed in the Land Titles Office for the Northwest Territories Land Registration District at Yellowknife under number 2310.
