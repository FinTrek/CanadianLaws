# Order Amalgamating and Combining the Privy Council Office with the Federal-Provincial Relations Office under the Prime Minister

**SI/93-126**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 14 July 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, is pleased hereby, pursuant to paragraph 2(b) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), to amalgamate and combine the Privy Council Office with the Federal-Provincial Relations Office under the Prime Minister and under the Clerk of the Privy Council and Secretary to the Cabinet.
