# Order Transferring to the Department of National Health and Welfare the Control and Supervision of Certain Portions of the Public Service in the Social Service Programs Branch of the Department of Employment and Immigration

**SI/93-167**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 25 August 1993

----------

Whereas, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), the control and supervision of certain portions of the public service were transferred from the Department of National Health and Welfare to the Department of Employment and Immigration by Order in Council P.C. 1993-1488 of June 25, 1993<sup><a href='#fn_SI-93-167_e_hq_6519'>[*]</a></sup>;

<a name='fn_SI-93-167_e_hq_6519'><sup>*</sup></a>: SI/93-142, 1993 Canada Gazette Part II, p. 3234<br />

Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), is pleased hereby to transfer to the Department of National Health and Welfare the control and supervision of those portions of the public service in the Social Service Programs Branch of the Department of Employment and Immigration known as the Family Violence Prevention Division, the Children’s Bureau and attendant Brighter Futures programs, the Community Action Program for Children, HIV/AIDS Research and Development and the Seniors Independence Research Program.
