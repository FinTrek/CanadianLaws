# Order Transferring to the Department of External Affairs the Control and Supervision of the Investment Development Division in Investment Canada

**SI/93-146**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 14 July 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), is pleased hereby to transfer to the Department of External Affairs the control and supervision of that portion of the public service in Investment Canada known as the Investment Development Division.
