# Order Designating the Minister of Communications as Minister for Purposes of Section 47 of the Act

**SI/93-232**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 15 December 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 47<sup><a href='#fn_SI-93-232_e_hq_6546'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md), is pleased hereby to designate the Minister of Communications, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the Minister for the purposes of section 47 of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md).

<a name='fn_SI-93-232_e_hq_6546'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1992, c. 54, s. 28<br />
