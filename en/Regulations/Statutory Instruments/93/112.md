# Order Designating the Minister of Supply and Services as Appropriate Minister with Respect to the Queens Quay West Land Corporation

**SI/93-112**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 14 July 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph (d) of the definition ***appropriate Minister*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), and subparagraph (a)(ii) of the definition of ***appropriate Minister*** in subsection 83(1) of that Act, is pleased hereby to designate the Minister of Supply and Services, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the appropriate Minister with respect to the Queens Quay West Land Corporation for the purposes of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md).
