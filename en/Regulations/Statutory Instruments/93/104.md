# Order Designating the Secretary of State of Canada as Appropriate Minister with Respect to the National Archives, the National Film Board, the National Library, the National Gallery, the National Museum of Science and Technology, the Canadian Museum of Civilization, and the Canadian Museum of Nature

**SI/93-104**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 14 July 1993

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, is pleased hereby

**(a)** pursuant to subsection 3(1.1) of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), to amend Schedule I.1 of that Act by striking out the reference to the Minister of Communications in column II thereof opposite the names of the National Archives of Canada, the National Film Board and the National Library in Column I thereof and by substituting therefor a reference to the Secretary of State of Canada in column II thereof opposite those names; and



**(b)** pursuant to paragraph (d) of the definition of ***appropriate Minister*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), and subparagraph (a)(ii) of the definition of ***appropriate Minister*** in subsection 83(1) of that Act, to designate the Secretary of State of Canada, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the appropriate Minister with respect to the National Gallery of Canada, the National Museum of Science and Technology, the Canadian Museum of Civilization, and the Canadian Museum of Nature.
