# Transfer of Duties Order

**SI/2017-10**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 22 February 2017

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)<sup><a href='#fn_612481-E_hq_7374'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)<sup><a href='#fn_612481-E_hq_7375'>[b]</a></sup>, transfers from the Minister of Industry to the Minister of State styled the Minister of Science the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Industry under Part I of the [Budget Implementation Act, 1997](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2026.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1338-E_hq_19113'>[c]</a></sup>.
