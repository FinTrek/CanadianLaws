# Order Designating the Deputy Minister of the Department of Indigenous Services Canada as Deputy Head in respect of that Entity

**SI/2017-78**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 13 December 2017

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(a)<sup><a href='#fn_608651-E_hq_152'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)<sup><a href='#fn_610261-E_hq_4314'>[b]</a></sup>, designates the Deputy Minister of the Department of Indigenous Services Canada as deputy head in respect of that entity, effective November 30, 2017.

<a name='fn_610261-E_hq_4314'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. F-11<br />
