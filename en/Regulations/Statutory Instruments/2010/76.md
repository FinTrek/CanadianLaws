# Order Acknowledging Receipt of the Assessments Done Pursuant to Subsection 23(1) of the Act

**SI/2010-76**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SPECIES AT RISK ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%2029.md)

Date registered: 13 October 2010

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment, hereby acknowledges receipt, on the making of this Order, of the assessments conducted under subsection 23(1) of the [Species at Risk Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%2029.md)<sup><a href='#fn_612991-E_hq_8959'>[a]</a></sup> by the Committee on the Status of Endangered Wildlife in Canada with respect to the species set out in the annexed schedule.

<a name='fn_612991-E_hq_8959'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2002, c. 29<br />
