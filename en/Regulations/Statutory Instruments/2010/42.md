# Order Acknowledging Receipt of the Assessments Done Pursuant to Subsection 23(1) of the Act

**SI/2010-42**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SPECIES AT RISK ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%2029.md)

Date registered: 9 June 2010

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment, hereby acknowledges receipt, on the making of this Order, of the Committee on the Status of Endangered Wildlife in Canada’s (COSEWIC) assessments under subsection 23(1) of the [Species at Risk Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%2029.md)<sup><a href='#fn_1500_ec_160'>[a]</a></sup> with respect to the species set out in the annexed schedule.

<a name='fn_1500_ec_160'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2002, c. 29<br />
