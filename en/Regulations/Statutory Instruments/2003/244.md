# Order Designating the Minister of Canadian Heritage as Minister for Purposes of the Act

**SI/2003-244**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PARLIAMENTARY EMPLOYMENT AND STAFF RELATIONS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2033%20(2nd%20Supp.).md)

Date registered: 31 December 2003

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, hereby designates the Minister of Canadian Heritage as the Minister for the purposes of the [Parliamentary Employment and Staff Relations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2033%20(2nd%20Supp.).md)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>, effective December 12, 2003.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: R.S., c. 33 (2nd Supp.)<br />
