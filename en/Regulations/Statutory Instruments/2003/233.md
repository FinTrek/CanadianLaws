# Order Transferring from the Department of Fisheries and Oceans to the Department of Transport of Certain Portions of the Directorate-General of Maritime Programs

**SI/2003-233**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 31 December 2003

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), hereby transfers from the Department of Fisheries and Oceans to the Department of Transport the control and supervision of those portions of the Directorate-General of Marine Programs that are responsible for policy related to pleasure craft safety, marine navigation services, pollution prevention and response, and navigable waters, effective December 12, 2003.
