# Order Designating the Chairperson of the Indian Residential Schools Truth and Reconciliation Commission as Deputy Head

**SI/2009-45**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 24 June 2009

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(a)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)<sup><a href='#footnoteb_e'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 8<br />

<a name='footnoteb_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. F-11<br />

**(a)** repeals Order in Council P.C. 2008-798 of April 25, 2008<sup><a href='#footnotec_e'>[c]</a></sup>, effective July 1, 2009;

<a name='footnotec_e'><sup>c</sup></a>: SI/2008-43<br />



**(b)** designates the Chairperson of the Indian Residential Schools Truth and Reconciliation Commission as deputy head in respect of that Commission, effective July 1, 2009; and



**(c)** repeals paragraph (b), effective July 1, 2015.
