# Order Transferring from the Minister of Employment and Immigration to the Minister of Labour, all the Powers, Duties and Functions of the Minister of Employment and Immigration under Certain Acts

**SI/95-36**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 22 March 1995

----------

Whereas, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), all the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Labour under any Act of Parliament were transferred to the Minister of Employment and Immigration by Order in Council P.C. 1993-1488 of June 25, 1993<sup><a href='#fn_SI-95-36_e_hq_6606'>[*]</a></sup>;

<a name='fn_SI-95-36_e_hq_6606'><sup>*</sup></a>: SI/93-142, 1993 Canada Gazette Part II, p. 3234<br />

Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), is pleased hereby to transfer from the Minister of Employment and Immigration to the Minister of Labour all the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Employment and Immigration under

**(a)** the [Canada Labour Code](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/L/L-2.md);



**(b)** the [Canadian Centre for Occupational Health and Safety Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-13.md);



**(c)** section 16 of the Corporations and Labour Unions Returns Act;



**(d)** the Department of Labour Act, other than sections 2 and 5;



**(e)** the [Fair Wages and Hours of Labour Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/L/L-4.md);



**(f)** the [Government Employees Compensation Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/G/G-5.md);



**(g)** paragraphs 28(2)(d) and 43(2)(a) of the [Hazardous Materials Information Review Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2024%20(3rd%20Supp.),%20Part%20III.md);



**(h)** [An Act respecting the Hudson Bay Mining and Smelting Co., Limited](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1947/c.%2062.md), chapter 62 of the Statutes of Canada, 1947;



**(i)** the [Merchant Seamen Compensation Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/M/M-6.md);



**(j)** section 9 of the [Non-smokers’ Health Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2015%20(4th%20Supp.).md);



**(k)** Part II of the [Status of the Artist Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1992/c.%2033.md);



**(l)** the [Wages Liability Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/W/W-1.md); and



**(m)** the [West Coast Ports Operations Act, 1994](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1994/c.%201.md).
