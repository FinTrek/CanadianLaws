# Order Transferring to the Department of Agriculture and Agri-Food the Control and Supervision of the Portion of the Public Service known as the Retail Food Program of the Department of Industry

**SI/95-85**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 9 August 1995

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), is pleased hereby to transfer to the Department of Agriculture and Agri-Food the control and supervision of the portion of the public service in the Department of Industry known as the Retail Food Program.
