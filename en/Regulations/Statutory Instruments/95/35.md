# Order Transferring from the Department of Employment and Immigration to the Department of National Revenue the Control and Supervision of those Portions of the Public Service in the Income Security Programs Branch relating to Child Tax Benefit Program and Special Allowances under the Children’s Special Allowances Act

**SI/95-35**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 22 March 1995

----------

Whereas, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), the control and supervision of the Income Security Programs Branch were transferred from the Department of National Health and Welfare to the Department of Employment and Immigration by Order in Council P.C. 1993-1488 of June 25, 1993<sup><a href='#fn_SI-95-35_e_hq_6605'>[*]</a></sup>;

<a name='fn_SI-95-35_e_hq_6605'><sup>*</sup></a>: SI/93-142, 1993 Canada Gazette Part II, p. 3234<br />

Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), is pleased hereby to transfer, effective August 28, 1995, to the Department of National Revenue the control and supervision of those portions of the public service in the Income Security Programs Branch of the Department of Employment and Immigration relating to

**(a)** the Child Tax Benefit Program, and



**(b)** special allowances under the [Children’s Special Allowances Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1992/c.%2048,%20Sch..md).
