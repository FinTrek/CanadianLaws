# Order Designating the Executive Director of the Public Appointments Commission Secretariat as Deputy Head

**SI/2006-63**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 3 May 2006

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(a)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), hereby designates the Executive Director of the Public Appointments Commission Secretariat as deputy head in respect of that Secretariat.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 8<br />
