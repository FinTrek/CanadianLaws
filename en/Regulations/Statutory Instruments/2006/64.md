# Order Designating the Public Appointments Commission Secretariat as a Department and the Executive Director as Deputy Head

**SI/2006-64**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 3 May 2006

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, hereby

**(a)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition department in subsection 2(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>, designates the Public Appointments Commission Secretariat as a department for the purposes of that Act; and



**(b)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition deputy head in subsection 2(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)<sup>[a]</sup>, designates the Executive Director of the Public Appointments Commission Secretariat as the deputy head of that Secretariat for the purposes of that Act.



<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, ss. 12 and 13<br />
