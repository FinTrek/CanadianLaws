# Order designating the Department of Employment and Social Development to provide support to the Minister of Seniors

**SI/2018-61**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SALARIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-3.md)

Date registered: 8 August 2018

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 5(1)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1658-E_hq_22391'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Salaries Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-3.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1658-E_hq_22392'>[b]</a></sup>, designates the Department of Employment and Social Development to provide support to the Minister of Seniors in the carrying out of that Minister’s responsibilities.

<a name='fn_81000-3-1658-E_hq_22391'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2018, c. 18, s. 3<br />

<a name='fn_81000-3-1658-E_hq_22392'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. S-3<br />
