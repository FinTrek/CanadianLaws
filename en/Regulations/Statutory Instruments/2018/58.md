# Order designating the Department of Industry to provide support to the Minister of Science

**SI/2018-58**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SALARIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-3.md)

Date registered: 8 August 2018

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 5(1)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1633-E_hq_22311'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Salaries Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-3.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1633-E_hq_22312'>[b]</a></sup>, designates the Department of Industry to provide support to the Minister of Science in the carrying out of that Minister’s responsibilities.

<a name='fn_81000-3-1633-E_hq_22312'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. S-3<br />
