# Order designating the Department of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness to provide support to the Minister of Border Security and Organized Crime Reduction

**SI/2018-62**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SALARIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-3.md)

Date registered: 8 August 2018

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 5(1)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1659-E_hq_22389'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Salaries Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-3.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1659-E_hq_22390'>[b]</a></sup>, designates the Department of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness to provide support to the Minister of Border Security and Organized Crime Reduction in the carrying out of that Minister’s responsibilities.

<a name='fn_81000-3-1659-E_hq_22390'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. S-3<br />
