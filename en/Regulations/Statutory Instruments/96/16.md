# Order Transferring from the Minister of Finance to the Minister of Industry, the Powers, Duties and Functions in Relation to Regional Economic Development in Quebec and the Control and Supervision of the Economic Development Agency of Canada for the Regions of Quebec

**SI/96-16**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 7 February 1996

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a) of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md), is pleased hereby to transfer from the Minister of Finance to the Minister of Industry

**(a)** all the powers, duties and functions referred to in paragraph (a) of Order in Council P.C. 1993-1907 of November 4, 1993<sup><a href='#fn_SI-96-16_e_hq_6555'>[a]</a></sup> and in Order in Council P.C. 1995-537 of March 29, 1995<sup><a href='#fn_SI-96-16_e_hq_6556'>[b]</a></sup>; and

<a name='fn_SI-96-16_e_hq_6555'><sup>a</sup></a>: SI/93-206<br />

<a name='fn_SI-96-16_e_hq_6556'><sup>b</sup></a>: SI/95-49<br />



**(b)** the control and supervision of that portion of the public service known as the Economic Development Agency of Canada for the Regions of Quebec.
