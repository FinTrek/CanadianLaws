# Order Designating the President of Shared Services Canada as Deputy Head in Respect of that Entity

**SI/2011-68**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 17 August 2011

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(a)<sup><a href='#fn_a'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)<sup><a href='#fn_b'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby designates the President of Shared Services Canada as deputy head in respect of that entity, effective August 4, 2011.

<a name='fn_a'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 8<br />

<a name='fn_b'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. F-11<br />
