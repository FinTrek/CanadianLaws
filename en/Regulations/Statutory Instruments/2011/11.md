# Order Acknowledging Receipt of the Assessment Done Pursuant to Subsection 23(1) of the Act

**SI/2011-11**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SPECIES AT RISK ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%2029.md)

Date registered: 16 February 2011

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment, hereby acknowledges receipt, on the making of this Order, of the assessment done pursuant to subsection 23(1) of the [Species at Risk Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2002/c.%2029.md)<sup><a href='#fn_1e'>[a]</a></sup> by the Committee on the Status of Endangered Wildlife in Canada (COSEWIC) with respect to the species set out in the annexed schedule.

<a name='fn_1e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2002, c. 29<br />




### **SCHEDULE** 
## Species of Special Concern
##### Mammals

Bear, Polar (*Ursus maritimus*)<br />- <i>Ours blanc</i>
