# Order Transferring from the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development to the Minister of Health the Control and Supervision of the Canadian Northern Economic Development Agency and Ordering the Minister of Health to Preside over the Agency

**SI/2011-94**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 7 December 2011

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, hereby

**(a)** pursuant to paragraph 2(a)<sup><a href='#fn_a'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)<sup><a href='#fn_b'>[b]</a></sup>, transfers from the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development to the Minister of Health the control and supervision of the portion of the federal public administration known as the Canadian Northern Economic Development Agency; and



**(b)** orders that the Minister of Health shall preside over the Canadian Northern Economic Development Agency.



<a name='fn_a'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 207<br />

<a name='fn_b'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. P-34<br />
