# Order Designating the Minister of Canadian Heritage for Purposes of Section 23 of the Act

**SI/2005-124**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 14 December 2005

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 23(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby designates the Minister of Canadian Heritage for the purposes of section 23 of that Act, effective December 31, 2005.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, ss. 12 and 13<br />
