# Transfer of Duties Order

**SI/2015-93**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 18 November 2015

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, effective November 4, 2015,

(a) pursuant to paragraph 2(a)<sup><a href='#fn_612645E_hq_7867'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)<sup><a href='#fn_612645E_hq_7868'>[b]</a></sup>, transfers to the Minister of Infrastructure, Communities and Intergovernmental Affairs from the President of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada the control and supervision of that portion of the federal public administration known as the Office of Infrastructure of Canada; and

<a name='fn_612645E_hq_7867'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 207<br />

<a name='fn_612645E_hq_7868'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. P-34<br />



(b) orders that the Minister of Infrastructure, Communities and Intergovernmental Affairs is to preside over the Office of Infrastructure of Canada.
