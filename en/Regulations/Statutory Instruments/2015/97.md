# Transfer of Duties Order

**SI/2015-97**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 18 November 2015

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)<sup><a href='#fn_SI-2015-97_e_hq_16321'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SI-2015-97_e_hq_16322'>[b]</a></sup>, transfers from the Minister of Transport to the Minister of Infrastructure, Communities and Intergovernmental Affairs the powers, duties and functions under the [Bridge To Strengthen Trade Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2012/c.%2031,%20s.%20179.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SI-2015-97_e_hq_16323'>[c]</a></sup>, effective November 4, 2015.
