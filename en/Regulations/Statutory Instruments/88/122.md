# Order Transferring to the Minister of Finance the Powers, Duties and Functions of the Minister of Supply and Services under Part I of the Currency Act

**SI/88-122**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 3 August 1988

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 2 of the Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act, is pleased hereby to transfer to the Minister of Finance the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Supply and Services under Part I of the Currency Act as transferred by Order in Council P.C. 1976-2857 of November 18, 1976.
