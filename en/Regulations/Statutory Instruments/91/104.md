# Secretary of State Authority to Prescribe Fees Order (Student Loans)

**SI/91-104**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 17 July 1991

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Secretary of State of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19.1(b)<sup><a href='#fn_SI_91-104_EN_hq_20089'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), is pleased hereby to authorize the Secretary of State of Canada to prescribe, by order, a fee based on a percentage of the amount of the loan, to be paid by full-time students to whom guaranteed student loans are made after July 31, 1991 under the [Canada Student Loans Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-23.md).

<a name='fn_SI_91-104_EN_hq_20089'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 24, s. 6<br />
