# Order Designating the Minister of Public Works and Government Services as the Minister for the purposes of the Act

**SI/2016-19**

Enabling authorities: 
- PUBLIC SERVICE LABOUR RELATIONS ACT

Date registered: 4 May 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to the definition ***Minister*** in subsection 2(1) of the Public Service Labour Relations Act<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-859-E_hq_13947'>[a]</a></sup>,

<a name='fn_81000-3-859-E_hq_13947'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 2<br />

**(a)** repeals Order in Council P.C. 2005-278 of March 4, 2005<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1107-E_hq_16217'>[b]</a></sup>; and

<a name='fn_81000-2-1107-E_hq_16217'><sup>b</sup></a>: SI/2005-17<br />



**(b)** designates the Minister of Public Works and Government Services, who is a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada and is not a member of the Treasury Board, as the Minister for the purposes of the Public Service Labour Relations Act<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-859-E_hq_13947'>[a]</a></sup>.
