# Order Designating the Honourable Patricia A. Hajdu to Co-ordinate Policy with respect to the Status of Women and Administer Related Programs

**SI/2016-43**

Enabling authorities: 
- APPROPRIATION ACT NO. 3, 1976

Date registered: 13 July 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to National Health and Welfare Vote No. 65 of the Schedule to Appropriation Act No. 3, 1976<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-859-E_hq_13947'>[a]</a></sup>,

<a name='fn_81000-3-859-E_hq_13947'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1974-75-76, c. 102<br />

**(a)** repeals Order in Council P.C. 2015-1249 of November 4, 2015<sup><a href='#fn_4772_hq_16335'>[b]</a></sup>; and

<a name='fn_4772_hq_16335'><sup>b</sup></a>: SI/2015-105<br />



**(b)** designates the Honourable Patricia A. Hajdu, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, to co-ordinate policy with respect to the status of women and administer related programs.
