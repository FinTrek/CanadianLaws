# Order Designating the Minister of Citizenship and Immigration as Minister for Purposes of the Act

**SI/2008-136**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADIAN MULTICULTURALISM ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2024%20(4th%20Supp.).md)

Date registered: 12 November 2008

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to the definition ***Minister*** in section 2 of the [Canadian Multiculturalism Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2024%20(4th%20Supp.).md)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: R.S., c-24 (4th Supp.)<br />

**(a)** repeals Order in Council P.C. 1991-850 of May 9, 1991<sup><a href='#footnoteb_e'>[b]</a></sup>, and

<a name='footnoteb_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: SI/91-75<br />



**(b)** designates the Minister of Citizenship and Immigration, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the Minister for the purposes of the [Canadian Multiculturalism Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%2024%20(4th%20Supp.).md),



effective October 30, 2008.
