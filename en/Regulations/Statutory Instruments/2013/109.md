# Transfer of Duties Order

**SI/2013-109**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE REARRANGEMENT AND TRANSFER OF DUTIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)

Date registered: 23 October 2013

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph 2(a)<sup><a href='#fn_612645E_hq_7867'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Public Service Rearrangement and Transfer of Duties Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-34.md)<sup><a href='#fn_612645E_hq_7868'>[b]</a></sup>, transfers to the Minister of Health

<a name='fn_612645E_hq_7867'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 207<br />

<a name='fn_612645E_hq_7868'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. P-34<br />

**(a)** effective October 7, 2013,

**(i)** all of the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food under the [Canadian Food Inspection Agency Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%206.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-753-E_hq_12644'>[c]</a></sup>, except the powers, duties or functions conferred on the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food under subsection 4(2) of that Act that do not relate to food safety,

<a name='fn_81000-3-753-E_hq_12644'><sup>c</sup></a>: S.C. 1997, c. 6<br />



**(ii)** all of the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food relating to food safety under any Act of Parliament, and



**(iii)** the control and supervision of the Canadian Food Inspection Agency; and





**(b)** effective on the day on which the [Safe Food for Canadians Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2012/c.%2024.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-753-E_hq_12670'>[d]</a></sup> comes into force under s. 111 of that Act, all of the powers, duties and functions of the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food relating to food safety under that Act.

<a name='fn_81000-3-753-E_hq_12670'><sup>d</sup></a>: S.C. 2012, c. 24<br />
