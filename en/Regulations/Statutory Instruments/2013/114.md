# Order Repealing Order in Council P.C. 1999-472 and Designating the Minister of Industry for the purposes of the Act

**SI/2013-114**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA SMALL BUSINESS FINANCING ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1998/c.%2036.md)

Date registered: 6 November 2013

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to the definition ***Minister*** in section 2 of the [Canada Small Business Financing Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1998/c.%2036.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1836-E_hq_12595'>[a]</a></sup>,

<a name='fn_81000-2-1836-E_hq_12595'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1998, c. 36<br />

**(a)** repeals Order in Council P.C. 1999-472 of March 18, 1999<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1836-E_hq_12596'>[b]</a></sup>; and

<a name='fn_81000-2-1836-E_hq_12596'><sup>b</sup></a>: SI/99-32<br />



**(b)** designates the Minister of Industry, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, as the Minister for the purposes of the [Canada Small Business Financing Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1998/c.%2036.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1836-E_hq_12595'>[a]</a></sup>.
