# Order Repealing Order in Council P.C. 2008-12 and Designating the Minister of Health as the appropriate Minister with respect to the Canadian Food Inspection Agency for the purposes of the Act

**SI/2013-110**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 23 October 2013

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to paragraph (c.1)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-754-E_hq_12658'>[a]</a></sup> of the definition ***appropriate Minister*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-754-E_hq_12659'>[b]</a></sup>,

<a name='fn_81000-3-754-E_hq_12658'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1992, c. 1, s. 69(2)<br />

<a name='fn_81000-3-754-E_hq_12659'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. F-11<br />

**(a)** repeals Order in Council P.C. 2008-12 of January 16, 2008<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-754-E_hq_12660'>[c]</a></sup>, and

<a name='fn_81000-3-754-E_hq_12660'><sup>c</sup></a>: SI/2008-4<br />



**(b)** designates the Minister of Health as the appropriate Minister with respect to the Canadian Food Inspection Agency for the purposes of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-754-E_hq_12659'>[b]</a></sup>,
