# Order Designating the National Transportation Act Review Commission as a Department and the Prime Minister as Appropriate Minister

**SI/92-39**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 11 March 1992

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, is pleased hereby

**(a)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition ***department*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), to designate the National Transportation Act Review Commission as a department for the purposes of that Act; and



**(b)** pursuant to paragraph (b) of the definition ***appropriate Minister*** in section 2 of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), to designate the Prime Minister as the appropriate Minister with respect to the National Transportation Act Review Commission.
