# Reservation to the Crown Waiver Order (Hay River, N.W.T.)

**SI/92-119**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TERRITORIAL LANDS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/T/T-7.md)

Date registered: 1 July 1992

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to paragraph 13(b) of the [Territorial Lands Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/T/T-7.md), is pleased hereby to order that all that portion of the lands described in the schedule hereto lying within 30.48 metres (100 feet) of the Ordinary High Water Mark of Great Slave Lake in the Northwest Territories be included in the sale of the said lands.




### **SCHEDULE** 
The whole of Log numbered 554 in Group numbered 814, Hay River, in the Northwest Territories, as said lot is shown on a plan of survey of record number 50535 in the Canada Lands Surveys Records at Ottawa, a copy of which is filed in the Land Titles Office for the Northwest Territories Land Registration District at Yellowknife under number 247.
