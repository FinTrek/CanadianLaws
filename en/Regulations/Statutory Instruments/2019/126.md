# Order Designating the Minister of Employment and Social Development, a Minister of the Crown, to be “the Minister” for the purposes of that Act

**SI/2019-126**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA DISABILITY SAVINGS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2007/c.%2035,%20s.%20136.md)

Date registered: 11 December 2019

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 4 of the [Canada Disability Savings Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2007/c.%2035,%20s.%20136.md)<sup><a href='#fn_320_hq_10667'>[a]</a></sup>,

**(a)** repeals Order in Council P.C. 2018-1021 of July 18, 2018<sup><a href='#fn_81000-3-1906_hq_26064'>[b]</a></sup>; and



**(b)** designates the Minister of Employment and Social Development, a Minister of the Crown, to be “the Minister” for the purposes of that Act.
