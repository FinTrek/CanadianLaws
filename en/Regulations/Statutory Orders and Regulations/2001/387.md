## Factoring Entity



**Definition of factoring entity**

**1** For the purpose of the definition ***factoring entity*** in subsections 464(1) of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md), 386(1) of the [Cooperative Credit Associations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2048.md), 490(1) of the [Insurance Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2047.md) and 449(1) of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md), and in subparagraph 522.22(1)(b)(i) of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md), ***factoring entity*** means an entity the activities of which are limited to acting as a factor in respect of accounts receivable, which activities include the raising of money for the purpose of acting as a factor and the lending of money while acting as a factor.
