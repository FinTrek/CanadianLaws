# Exemption from Restrictions on Investments (Trust and Loan Companies) Regulations

**SOR/2001-386**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TRUST AND LOAN COMPANIES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)

Date registered: 4 October 2001

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to paragraphs 459(a)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2001-386_e_hq_6013'>[a]</a></sup> and (b)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2001-386_e_hq_6013'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2001-386_e_hq_6015'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Exemption from Restrictions on Investments (Trust and Loan Companies) Regulations.

<a name='fn_SOR-2001-386_e_hq_6013'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2001, c. 9, s. 550<br />

<a name='fn_SOR-2001-386_e_hq_6015'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 45<br />




## Exemption from Restrictions on Investments



**Prescribed circumstances**

**1** For the purposes of subparagraph 453(3)(f)(ii) of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md), subsections 453(4) to (6) of the Act do not apply in determining whether a company would be permitted to acquire a substantial investment in an entity under subsection 451(2), paragraph 451(3)(b) or (c) or subsection 451(4) or 453(1) or (2) of the Act.




## Coming into Force



**Coming into force**

<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2001-386_e_hq_6016'>[*]</a></sup>**2** These Regulations come into force on the day on which paragraphs 459(a) and (b) of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md), as enacted by section 550 of the [Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2001/c.%209.md), chapter 9 of the Statutes of Canada, 2001, come into force.
