## Exemption from Restrictions on Investments



**Prescribed circumstances — life companies and insurance holding companies**

**1** For the purposes of subparagraph 495(3)(d)(ii) and 971(3)(d)(ii) of the [Insurance Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2047.md), subsections 495(6) to (8) of the Act do not apply in determining whether a company would be permitted to acquire a substantial investment in an entity under subsection 493(2), paragraph 493(3)(b) or (c) or subsection 493(4) or 495(1) or (2) of the Act.




**Prescribed circumstances — property and casualty companies and marine companies**

**2** For the purpose of subparagraph 495(5)(d)(ii) of the [Insurance Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2047.md), subsections 495(6) to (8) of the Act do not apply in determining whether a company would be permitted to acquire a substantial investment in an entity under subsection 493(2), paragraph 493(3)(b) or (c) or subsection 495(1) or (4) of the Act.
> SOR/2009-296, s. 29.





**Prescribed circumstances — societies**

**3** For the purpose of subparagraph 554(3)(d)(ii) of the [Insurance Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2047.md), subsections 554(4) and (5) of the Act do not apply in determining whether a society would be permitted to acquire a substantial investment in an entity under subsection 552(2), paragraph 552(3)(b) or (c) or subsection 554(1) or (2) of the Act.
