# Equity of a Trust and Loan Company Regulations

**SOR/2001-379**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TRUST AND LOAN COMPANIES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)

Date registered: 4 October 2001

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 531<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2001-379_e_hq_5997'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2001-379_e_hq_5998'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Equity of a Trust and Loan Company Regulations.

<a name='fn_SOR-2001-379_e_hq_5997'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2001, c. 9, s. 569<br />

<a name='fn_SOR-2001-379_e_hq_5998'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 45<br />




## Equity of a Company



**Equity**

**1** For the purposes of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md), the equity of a company is the sum of the shareholders’ equity of the company and the minority interests in entities controlled by the company, as they appear in the consolidated financial statements of the company.




## Coming into Force



**Coming into force**

<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2001-379_e_hq_5999'>[*]</a></sup>**2** These Regulations come into force on the day on which paragraph 531(f.1) of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md), as enacted by section 569 of the [Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2001/c.%209.md), chapter 9 of the Statutes of Canada, 2001, comes into force.
