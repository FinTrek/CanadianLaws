# Prohibited Activities Respecting Real Property (Foreign Banks) Regulations

**SOR/2001-411**

Enabling authorities: 
- [BANK ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md)

Date registered: 4 October 2001

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 978<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md)<sup><a href='#footnoteb_e'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Prohibited Activities Respecting Real Property (Foreign Banks) Regulations.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2001, c. 9, s. 183<br />

<a name='footnoteb_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 46<br />




## Prohibited Activities



**Prohibited activities**

**1** Paragraphs 510(1)(a) and (b) of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md) apply in respect of the holding, managing and other dealing with real property in Canada by a foreign bank or an entity associated with a foreign bank if that holding, managing or other dealing involves the making or acquiring of loans or advances on security of real property in Canada.




## Coming into Force



**Coming into force**

<sup><a href='#footnote1star_e'>[*]</a></sup>**2** These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 514 of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md), as enacted by section 132 of the [Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2001/c.%209.md), chapter 9 of the Statutes of Canada, 2001, comes into force.

<a name='footnote1star_e'><sup>*</sup></a>: [Note: Regulations in force October 24, 2001, *see* SI/2001-102.]<br />
