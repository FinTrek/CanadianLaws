# Security Certificate Transfer Fee (Banks, Bank Holding Companies, Insurance Companies and Insurance Holding Companies) Regulations

**SOR/2001-426**

Enabling authorities: 
- [BANK ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md)

- [INSURANCE COMPANIES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2047.md)

Date registered: 4 October 2001

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to sections 978<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md)<sup><a href='#footnoteb_e'>[b]</a></sup> and 1021<sup><a href='#footnotec_e'>[c]</a></sup> of the [Insurance Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2047.md)<sup><a href='#footnoted_e'>[d]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Security Certificate Transfer Fee (Banks, Bank Holding Companies, Insurance Companies and Insurance Holding Companies) Regulations.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2001, c. 9, s. 183<br />

<a name='footnoteb_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 46<br />

<a name='footnotec_e'><sup>c</sup></a>: S.C. 2001, c. 9, s. 465<br />

<a name='footnoted_e'><sup>d</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 47<br />




## Maximum Fee



**Maximum fee**

**1** For the purposes of subsections 85(2) of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md) and 89(2) of the [Insurance Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2047.md), the maximum fee that may be charged for a security certificate issued in respect of a transfer is $5.
