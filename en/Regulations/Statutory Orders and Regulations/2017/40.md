# Order Designating New Brunswick for the Purposes of the Criminal Interest Rate Provisions of the Criminal Code

**SOR/2017-40**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CRIMINAL CODE](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-46.md)

Date registered: 24 March 2017

----------

Whereas New Brunswick has legislative measures that protect recipients of payday loans and that provide for limits on the total cost of borrowing under a payday loan agreement;

And whereas the Lieutenant Governor in Council of New Brunswick has requested that the Governor in Council designate that province for the purposes of section 347.1<sup><a href='#fn_610628-e_hq_3365'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Criminal Code](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-46.md)<sup><a href='#fn_610628-e_hq_3366'>[b]</a></sup>;

Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Justice and the Minister of Industry, pursuant to subsection 347.1(3)<sup><a href='#fn_610628-e_hq_3365'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Criminal Code](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-46.md)<sup><a href='#fn_610628-e_hq_3366'>[b]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Order Designating New Brunswick for the Purposes of the Criminal Interest Rate Provisions of the Criminal Code.

<a name='fn_610628-e_hq_3365'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2007, c. 9, s. 2<br />

<a name='fn_610628-e_hq_3366'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. C-46<br />




## Province Designated


**1** New Brunswick is designated for the purposes of section 347.1 of the [Criminal Code](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-46.md).




## Coming into Force


<sup><a href='#fn_Ind7E94_hq_19927'>[*]</a></sup>**2** This Order comes into force at 12:00 a.m. Atlantic time on the first day on which the following are both in force:

- **(b)** Payday Lending Regulation – Cost of Credit Disclosure and Payday Loans Act.
