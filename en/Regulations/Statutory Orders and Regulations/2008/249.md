# Special Economic Measures (Zimbabwe) Permit Authorization Order

**SOR/2008-249**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SPECIAL ECONOMIC MEASURES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1992/c.%2017.md)

Date registered: 4 September 2008

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to subsection 4(4) of the [Special Economic Measures Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1992/c.%2017.md)<sup><a href='#fn_612090-E_hq_5146'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby authorizes the Minister of Foreign Affairs to issue to any person in Canada or any Canadian outside Canada a permit to carry out a specified activity or transaction, or any class of activity or transaction, that is restricted or prohibited pursuant to the [Special Economic Measures (Zimbabwe) Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2008/248.md).

<a name='fn_612090-E_hq_5146'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1992, c. 17<br />
