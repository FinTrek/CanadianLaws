# Returnable Beverage Container (GST/HST) Regulations

**SOR/2008-48**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXCISE TAX ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md)

Date registered: 28 February 2008

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to paragraph 226(2)(b)<sup><a href='#fna_e'>[a]</a></sup> and section 277<sup><a href='#fnb_e'>[b]</a></sup> of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), hereby makes the annexed Returnable Beverage Container (GST/HST) Regulations.

<a name='fna_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2007, c. 18, s. 28(2)<br />

<a name='fnb_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1993, c. 27, s. 125(1)<br />




**Prescribed Acts**

**1** For the purposes of paragraph 226(2)(b) of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), the following Acts are prescribed:
- **(a)** the Environment Act, S.N.S. 1994-95, c. 1;
- **(b)** the Beverage Containers Act, R.S.N.B. 2011, c. 121;
- **(c)** the Beverage Containers Act, R.S.P.E.I. 1988, c. B-2.1; and
- **(d)** the Environmental Protection Act, S.N.L. 2002, c. E-14.2.
