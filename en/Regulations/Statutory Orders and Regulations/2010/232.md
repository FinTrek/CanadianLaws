# Annual Statement (Trust and Loan Companies) Regulations

**SOR/2010-232**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TRUST AND LOAN COMPANIES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)

Date registered: 28 October 2010

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance pursuant to subsections 313(2)<sup><a href='#fn_1a'>[a]</a></sup> and 531(1)<sup><a href='#fn_1b'>[b]</a></sup> of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)<sup><a href='#fn_1c'>[c]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Annual Statement (Trust and Loan Companies) Regulations.

<a name='fn_1a'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2005, c. 54, s. 434<br />

<a name='fn_1b'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 2005, c. 54, s. 449<br />

<a name='fn_1c'><sup>c</sup></a>: S.C, 1991, c. 45<br />




## Contents of Annual Statement


**1** The prescribed statements referred to in subsection 313(2) of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md) are
- **(a)** a balance sheet as at the end of the financial year;
- **(b)** a statement of income for the financial year;
- **(c)** a statement of cash flows for the financial year;
- **(d)** a statement of changes in shareholders’ equity for the financial year; and
- **(e)** a statement of comprehensive income for the financial year.
> SOR/2014-273, s. 46(F); SOR/2015-32, s. 1.





## Coming into Force


**2** These Regulations come into force on June 1, 2011.
