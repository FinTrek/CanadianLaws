# Exemption for Public Notices or Documents (Cooperative Credit Associations) Regulations

**SOR/2010-237**

Enabling authorities: 
- [COOPERATIVE CREDIT ASSOCIATIONS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2048.md)

Date registered: 28 October 2010

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 463(1)<sup><a href='#fn_1a'>[a]</a></sup> and section 487.14<sup><a href='#fn_1b'>[b]</a></sup> of the [Cooperative Credit Associations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2048.md)<sup><a href='#fn_1c'>[c]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Exemption for Public Notices or Documents Regulations (Cooperative Credit Associations) Regulations.

<a name='fn_1a'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2005, c. 54, s. 208<br />

<a name='fn_1b'><sup>b</sup></a>: S C. 2005, c. 54, s. 212<br />

<a name='fn_1c'><sup>c</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 48<br />




## Interpretation


**1** In these Regulations, ***Act*** means the [Cooperative Credit Associations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2048.md).




## Exemption Circumstances


**2** For the purpose of section 487.14 of the Act, the prescribed circumstances are that the exemption does not prejudice any of the members or the shareholders or the public interest.




## Coming into Force


**3** These Regulations come into force on June 1, 2011.
