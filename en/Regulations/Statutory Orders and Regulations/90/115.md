# Order Prescribing the Fees To Be Paid by Persons to whom Services or the Use of Facilities is Provided by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police at the R.C.M.P. Training Academy in Regina, Saskatchewan

**SOR/90-115**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 2 February 1990

----------

The Solicitor General of Canada, pursuant to Order in Council P.C. 1989-1898 of September 21, 1989<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-90-115_e_hq_5204'>[*]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Order prescribing the fees to be paid by persons to whom services or the use of facilities is provided by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police at the R.C.M.P. Training Academy in Regina, Saskatchewan.

<a name='fn_SOR-90-115_e_hq_5204'><sup>*</sup></a>: SI/89-214, 1989 Canada Gazette Part II, p. 4298<br />

Ottawa, January 26, 1990


<p>PIERRE BLAIS<br />*Solicitor General of Canada*<br /></p>




## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Training Academy Fees Order.




## Fees


**2** Any person to whom the Royal Canadian Mounted Police provide one of the following services or the use of one of the following facilities at the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Training Academy in Regina, Saskatchewan, shall pay the following fees:
- **(a)** in the case of academic training provided in a classroom by an RCMP instructor, $31 per hour;
- **(b)** in the case of gas and small arms training, $24 per hour;
- **(c)** in the case of driver training provided by an RCMP instructor using an RCMP vehicle, $31 per hour;
- **(d)** in the case of accommodation $17 per day; and
- **(e)** in the case of the use of a classroom by a person other than an RCMP instructor, $17 per hour.
