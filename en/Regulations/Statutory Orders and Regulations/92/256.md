# Regulations Prescribing the Maximum Fee that a Company May Charge for a Security Certificate Issued in Respect of a Transfer

**SOR/92-256**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TRUST AND LOAN COMPANIES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)

Date registered: 7 May 1992

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 88(2) and section 531 of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)<sup><a href='#fn_1e'>[*]</a></sup>, is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations prescribing the maximum fee that a company may charge for a security certificate issued in respect of a transfer, effective June 1, 1992.

<a name='fn_1e'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 45<br />




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Security Certificate Transfer Fee (Trust and Loan Companies) Regulations.




## Maximum Fee


**2** A company may charge a fee not exceeding five dollars for a security certificate issued in respect of a transfer.
