# Regulations Exempting Animals from the Application of Section 19 of the Health of Animals Act

**SOR/91-3**

Enabling authorities: 
- [HEALTH OF ANIMALS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1990/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 13 December 1990

----------

The Minister of Agriculture, pursuant to subsection 19(6) of the [Health of Animals Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1990/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-91-3_e_hq_5222'>[*]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Regulations exempting animals from the application of section 19 of the Health of Animals Act.

<a name='fn_SOR-91-3_e_hq_5222'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1990, c. 21<br />

Dated at Ottawa, the 10th day of December, 1990


<p>DON MAZANKOWSKI<br />*Minister of Agriculture*<br /></p>




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Export Inspection and Certification Exemption Regulations.




## Exemptions


**2** All animals are exempt from the application of section 19 of the [Health of Animals Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1990/c.%2021.md) except for horses, swine, foxes, mink, members of the suborder *rumenatiae* and members of the family *camelidae*.
