# Regulations Prescribing Medical Devices

**SOR/91-24**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXCISE TAX ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md)

Date registered: 18 December 1990

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1)<sup><a href='#footnotestar_e'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations prescribing medical devices.

<a name='footnotestar_e'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1990, c. 45, s. 12<br />




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Medical Devices (GST) Regulations.




## Prescribed Property


**2** The following property is prescribed for the purposes of section 31 of Part II of Schedule VI to the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md):
- **(a) to (d)** [Repealed, SOR/99-171, s. 1]
