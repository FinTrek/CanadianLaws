# Regulations Prescribing Taxable Goods Imported by Mail or Courier

**SOR/91-32**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXCISE TAX ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md)

Date registered: 18 December 1990

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-91-32_e_hq_6140'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations prescribing taxable goods imported by mail or courier.

<a name='fn_SOR-91-32_e_hq_6140'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1990, c. 45, s. 12<br />




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Mail and Courier Imports (GST/HST) Regulations.
> SOR/2001-63, s. 1.





## Interpretation


**2** In these Regulations, ***Act*** means the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md). (*Loi*)




## Prescribed Goods


**3** For the purposes of section 7 of Schedule VII to the Act, the following imported goods are prescribed goods:
- **(a)** excisable goods and wine;
- **(b)** goods that are prescribed property for the purposes of section 143.1 of the Act, if the supplier of the goods is required to register under Subdivision d of Division V of Part IX of the Act and is not so registered;
- **(c)** goods for which the value for duty is reduced by the application of section 85 of the [Customs Tariff](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md);
- **(d)** goods that are purchased from a retailer in Canada and mailed or transported from outside Canada directly to the purchaser; and
- **(e)** goods that are purchased or ordered through or from a person in Canada acting for or on behalf of a person outside Canada who is selling the goods.
> SOR/2001-63, s. 2.
