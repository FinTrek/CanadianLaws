# School Cafeteria Food and Beverages (GST/HST) Regulations

**SOR/91-29**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXCISE TAX ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md)

Date registered: 18 December 1990

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-91-29_e_hq_6137'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations prescribing taxable school cafeteria food and beverages.

<a name='fn_SOR-91-29_e_hq_6137'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1990, c. 45, s. 12<br />



**1** [Repealed, SOR/2002-277, s. 7]




## Interpretation


**2** In these Regulations, ***Act*** means the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md). (*Loi*)




## Prescribed Food and Beverages


**3** For the purposes of section 12 of Part III of Schedule V to the Act, the following are prescribed food and beverages:
- **(a)** carbonated beverages;
- **(b)** beverages included in paragraph 1(d) of Part III of Schedule VI to the Act, when sold in cans, cartons or bottles; and
- **(c)** foods included in any of paragraphs 1(e) to (l) of Part III of Schedule VI to the Act, when prepackaged for sale to consumers.
