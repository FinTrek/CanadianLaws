# Equivalent Courses (GST/HST) Regulations

**SOR/91-27**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXCISE TAX ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md)

Date registered: 18 December 1990

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-91-27_e_hq_6136'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations prescribing equivalents of courses that follow a curriculum designated by a school authority.

<a name='fn_SOR-91-27_e_hq_6136'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1990, c. 45, s. 12<br />



**1** [Repealed, SOR/2002-277, s. 5]




## Prescribed Courses


**2** For the purpose of section 9 of Part III of Schedule V to the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), music lessons are prescribed equivalents of music courses that follow a curriculum designated by a school authority.
> SOR/94-368, s. 4.
