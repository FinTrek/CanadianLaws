# Regulations Respecting the Disclosure of Social Insurance Numbers for the Purpose of Registration under Section 240 of the Excise Tax Act

**SOR/91-41**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXCISE TAX ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md)

Date registered: 18 December 1990

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 277(1)(d)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-91-41_e_hq_6150'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations respecting the disclosure of Social Insurance Numbers for the purpose of registration under section 240 of the Excise Tax Act.

<a name='fn_SOR-91-41_e_hq_6150'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1990, c. 45, s. 12(1)<br />




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Social Insurance Number Disclosure Regulations.




## Disclosure Requirement


**2** Every individual who applies to the Minister to be registered pursuant to section 240 of the [Excise Tax Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-15.md) in the form prescribed by the Minister for that purpose shall disclose therein the individual’s Social Insurance Number.
