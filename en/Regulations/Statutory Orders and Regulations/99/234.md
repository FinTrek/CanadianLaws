# Regulations Designating Certain Countries for the Purposes of Tariff Item No. 9810.00.00, 1999-1

Enabling authorities: 
- [CUSTOMS TARIFF](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md)
