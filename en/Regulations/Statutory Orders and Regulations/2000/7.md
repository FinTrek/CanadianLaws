# Offshore Minerals Revenue Election Regulations

**SOR/2000-7**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FEDERAL-PROVINCIAL FISCAL ARRANGEMENTS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1970/c.%20F-6.md)

Date registered: 16 December 1999

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 4(11)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2000-7_e_hq_3619'>[a]</a></sup> and paragraph 40(g) of the [Federal-Provincial Fiscal Arrangements Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1970/c.%20F-6.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2000-7_e_hq_3621'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Offshore Minerals Revenue Election Regulations.

<a name='fn_SOR-2000-7_e_hq_3619'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1999, c. 11, s. 2(6)<br />

<a name='fn_SOR-2000-7_e_hq_3621'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1995, c. 17, s. 45(1)<br />




## Election


**1** A province referred to in subsection 4(11) of the [Federal-Provincial Fiscal Arrangements Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1970/c.%20F-6.md) shall make the election referred to in that subsection by informing the Minister of Finance of that election in writing.




## Coming into Force


**2** These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
