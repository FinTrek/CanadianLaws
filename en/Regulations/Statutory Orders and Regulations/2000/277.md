# Deposit Insurance Application Fee By-law

**SOR/2000-277**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA DEPOSIT INSURANCE CORPORATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-3.md)

Date registered: 29 June 2000

----------

The Board of Directors of the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(g)<sup><a href='#Footnote1_e'>[a]</a></sup> and subsection 18(1)<sup><a href='#Footnote2_e'>[b]</a></sup> of the [Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-3.md), hereby makes the annexed Deposit Insurance Application Fee By-law.

June 29, 2000



<a name='Footnote1_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: R.S. c. 18 (3rd Supp.), s. 51<br />

<a name='Footnote2_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1999, c. 28, s. 100<br />


## Prescribed Fee


**1** The prescribed fee that is to accompany an application for deposit insurance made by a provincial institution under subsection 17(1.1) of the [Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/C/C-3.md) is $20,000 and is non-refundable.
> SOR/2006-235, s. 1.





## Coming into Force


**2** This By-law comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
