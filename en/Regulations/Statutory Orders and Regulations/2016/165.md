# Candles Regulations

**SOR/2016-165**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 22 June 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81200-2-TEXTILE_hc'>[a]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Candles Regulations.

<a name='fn_81200-2-TEXTILE_hc'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2010, c. 21<br />




## Requirement



**Spontaneous reignition**

**1** A candle must not reignite spontaneously after it is extinguished.

**Registration**

**3** These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
