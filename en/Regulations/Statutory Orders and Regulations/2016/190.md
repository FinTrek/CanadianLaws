# Residential Detectors Regulations

**SOR/2016-190**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 22 June 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2016-190_e_hq_18685'>[a]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Residential Detectors Regulations.

<a name='fn_SOR-2016-190_e_hq_18685'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2010, c. 21<br />

**3** The information required under these Regulations to be provided on the label of a residential detector or in instructions intended for consumers must appear in English and in French.
