# Infant Feeding Bottle Nipples Regulations

**SOR/2016-180**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 22 June 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81200-2-TEXTILE_hc'>[a]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Infant Feeding Bottle Nipples Regulations.

<a name='fn_81200-2-TEXTILE_hc'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2010, c. 21<br />




## Interpretation



**Definition of infant feeding bottle nipple**

**1** In these Regulations, ***infant feeding bottle nipple*** includes a similar product that is put in the mouth of an infant during use.




## Requirement



**Limit of volatile N-nitrosamines**

**2** An infant feeding bottle nipple must not contain more than 10 µg/kg total volatile N-nitrosamines, as determined by dichloromethane extraction.
