# Ice Hockey Helmet Regulations

**SOR/2016-186**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 22 June 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81200-2-TEXTILE_hc'>[a]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Ice Hockey Helmet Regulations.

<a name='fn_81200-2-TEXTILE_hc'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2010, c. 21<br />




## Requirement



**Standard**

**1** Ice hockey helmets must meet the requirements of standard Z262.1, entitled Ice hockey helmets, published by the Canadian Standards Association, as amended from time to time.

## Coming into Force



**Registration**

**3** These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
