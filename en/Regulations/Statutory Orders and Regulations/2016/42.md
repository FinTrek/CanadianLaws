# Order Extending the Application of the Freezing Assets of Corrupt Foreign Officials (Tunisia) Regulations

**SOR/2016-42**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FREEZING ASSETS OF CORRUPT FOREIGN OFFICIALS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2011/c.%2010.md)

Date registered: 11 March 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to section 6 of the [Freezing Assets of Corrupt Foreign Officials Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2011/c.%2010.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-2665-E_hq_16607'>[a]</a></sup>, extends the application of the [Freezing Assets of Corrupt Foreign Officials (Tunisia) Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2011/78.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-2665-E_hq_16608'>[b]</a></sup> for a period of five years beginning on March 24, 2016.
