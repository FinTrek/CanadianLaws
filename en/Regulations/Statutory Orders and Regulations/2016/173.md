# Face Protectors for Ice Hockey and Box Lacrosse Players Regulations

**SOR/2016-173**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 22 June 2016

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81200-2-TEXTILE'>[a]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Face Protectors for Ice Hockey and Box Lacrosse Players Regulations.

<a name='fn_81200-2-TEXTILE'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2010, c. 21<br />




## Requirement



**Standard**

**1** Face protectors for ice hockey and box lacrosse players must meet the requirements of standard CAN3-Z262.2-M78, entitled Face Protectors for Ice Hockey and Box Lacrosse Players, published by the Canadian Standards Association in English in December 1978 and in French in June 1979.
