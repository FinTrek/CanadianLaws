# Transfer of the Crown Corporation Secretariat Regulations (Royal Canadian Mint and Canada Post Corporation)

**SOR/2006-36**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 6 February 2006

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 123(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Transfer of the Crown Corporation Secretariat Regulations (Royal Canadian Mint and Canada Post Corporation).

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, ss. 12, 13<br />




## Block Transfer


**1** Subsections 132(1) and (2) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)<sup><a href='#footnote1_e'>[1]</a></sup> apply to all persons employed or engaged in the portion of the federal public administration within the Canada Revenue Agency known as the Crown Corporation Secretariat, relating to the Royal Canadian Mint and the Canada Post Corporation.

<a name='footnote1_e'><sup>1</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, ss. 12, 13<br />




## Coming into Force


**2** These Regulations come into force on February 6, 2006.
