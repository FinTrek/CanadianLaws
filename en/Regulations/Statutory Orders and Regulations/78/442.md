# Regulations Respecting the Application of the Public Service Employment act to Employees of the Prairie Farm Rehabilitation Administration

**SOR/78-442**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 12 May 1978

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Regional Economic Expansion, pursuant to subsection 34(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations respecting the application of the Public Service Employment Act to employees of the Prairie Farm Rehabilitation Administration.




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Prairie Farm Rehabilitation Administration Employees Regulations.




## Application of the Public Service Employment Act


**2** Notwithstanding subsection 6(2) of the [Prairie Farm Rehabilitation Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-17.md), the provisions of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md) apply to all officers and employees, other than seasonal or term employees, required for the carrying out of the [Prairie Farm Rehabilitation Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/P/P-17.md).
