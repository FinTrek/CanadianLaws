# Order Prescribing the Fees or Charges To Be Paid by Participants to One-Day Local Employer Seminars Hosted by Canada Employment Centres Across Canada

**SOR/86-1007**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 26 September 1986

----------

The Minister of Employment and Immigration, pursuant to Order in Council P.C. 1986-2103 of September 11, 1986<sup><a href='#footnote_e'>[*]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Order prescribing the fees or charges to be paid by participants to one-day local employer seminars hosted by Canada Employment Centres across Canada.

<a name='footnote_e'><sup>*</sup></a>: SI/86-179, 1986 Canada Gazette Part II, p. 4245<br />

Ottawa, September 23, 1986


<p>BENOIT BOUCHARD<br />*Minister of Employment and Immigration*<br /></p>




## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the One-Day Local Employment Seminars Fee or Charge Order.




## Fee or Charge


**2** The fee or charge to be paid by each participant to the one-day local employer seminars hosted by Canada Employment Centres across Canada is $25.
