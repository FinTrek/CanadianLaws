# Rules Respecting Qualifications for Appointment of a Member, other than an Officer, in the Royal Canadian Mounted Police

**SOR/88-366**

Enabling authorities: 
- [ROYAL CANADIAN MOUNTED POLICE ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/R/R-10.md)

Date registered: 30 June 1988

----------

The Commissioner of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police, pursuant to subsection 10(1)<sup><a href='#fn_1e'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Royal Canadian Mounted Police Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/R/R-10.md), hereby makes the annexed Rules respecting qualifications for appointment of a member, other than an officer, in the Royal Canadian Mounted Police, effective June 30, 1988.

<a name='fn_1e'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1986, c. 11., s. 4<br />

Ottawa, June 30, 1988




## Short Title


**1** These Rules may be cited as Commissioner’s Standing Orders (Qualifications).
> SOR/90-102, s. 2(F).





## Qualifications
> [SOR/90-102, s. 3(F)]



**2** In addition to the qualifications noted in section 9.1 of the [Royal Canadian Mounted Police Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/R/R-10.md), no person shall be appointed to be a member, other than an officer, unless that person is
- **(a)** proficient in either official language, and
- **(b)** 19 years of age or older.
> SOR/90-102, s. 4.
