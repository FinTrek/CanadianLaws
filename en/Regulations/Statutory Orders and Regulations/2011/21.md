# Ice Hockey Helmet Regulations

**SOR/2011-21**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 4 February 2011

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_1e'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Ice Hockey Helmet Regulations.

<a name='fn_1e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2010, c. 21<br />




## Application



**Scope**

**1** These Regulations apply to the importation, advertising and sale of ice hockey helmets.




## Requirement



**Standard**

**2** Ice hockey helmets must meet the requirements of Standard Z262.1, entitled Ice hockey helmets, published by the Canadian Standards Association, as amended from time to time.




## Coming into Force



**S.C. 2010, c. 21**

<sup><a href='#fn_Ind1F6_hq_10474'>[*]</a></sup>**3** These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md) comes into force.
