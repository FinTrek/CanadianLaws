# Restraint Systems and Booster Seats for Motor Vehicles Regulations

**SOR/2011-16**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)

Date registered: 4 February 2011

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the [Canada Consumer Product Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2010/c.%2021.md)<sup><a href='#fn_1e'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Restraint Systems and Booster Seats for Motor Vehicles Regulations.

<a name='fn_1e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2010, c. 21<br />




## Application



**Scope**

**1** These Regulations apply to the importation, advertising and sale of restraint systems and booster seats for motor vehicles.




## Requirement



**General**

**2** Restraint systems and booster seats described in column 1 of the table to this section must meet the specifications set out in the Parts of the [Motor Vehicle Restraint Systems and Booster Seats Safety Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2010/90.md) that are mentioned in column 2.
