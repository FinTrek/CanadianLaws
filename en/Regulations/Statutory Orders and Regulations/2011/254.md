# Transfer of the Communications Security Establishment in the Department of National Defence Regulations

**SOR/2011-254**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 15 November 2011

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to subsection 123(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2011-254_e_hq_11581'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Transfer of the Communications Security Establishment in the Department of National Defence Regulations.

<a name='fn_SOR-2011-254_e_hq_11581'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, ss. 12 and 13<br />




## Block Transfer


**1** Subsection 132(1) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md) applies to all persons employed or engaged in the portion of the federal public administration in the Department of National Defence known as the Communications Security Establishment.




## Coming into Force


**2** These Regulations come into force on November 16, 2011.
