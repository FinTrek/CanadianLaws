# General Import Permit No. 81 — Specialty Steel Products

## General


**1** Any resident of Canada may, under the authority of this Permit, import into Canada any goods described in item 81 of the [Import Control List](/en/Regulations/Consolidated%20Regulations%20of%20Canada/601-700/C.R.C.,%20c.%20604.md).



**2** If any goods imported under the authority of this Permit are required to be reported in the prescribed form under the [Customs Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%201%20(2nd%20Supp.).md), that form must contain the statement “Imported under the authority of General Import Permit No. 81 — Specialty Steel Products” or “*Importé en vertu de la Licence générale d’importation no 81 — Acier ordinaire*”.
