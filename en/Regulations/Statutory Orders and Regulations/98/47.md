# Tariff Item Nos. 9971.00.00 and 9992.00.00 Accounting Regulations

**SOR/98-47**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CUSTOMS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%201%20(2nd%20Supp.).md)

Date registered: 29 December 1997

----------

Whereas the proposed Tariff Item Nos. 9971.00.00 and 9992.00.00 Accounting Regulations give effect, in part, to a public announcement made on October 27, 1997 and are therefore, by virtue of paragraph 164(4)(a.2)<sup><a href='#footnotea'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Customs Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%201%20(2nd%20Supp.).md)<sup><a href='#footnoteb'>[b]</a></sup>, not required to be published under subsection 164(3) of that Act;

<a name='footnoteb'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. 1 (2nd Supp.)<br />

Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to subsections 32(1)<sup><a href='#footnotec'>[c]</a></sup>, (2) and (5)<sup><a href='#footnoted'>[d]</a></sup> and paragraph 164(1)(i)<sup><a href='#footnotee'>[e]</a></sup> of the [Customs Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%201%20(2nd%20Supp.).md)<sup>[b]</sup>, hereby makes the annexed Tariff Item Nos. 9971.00.00 and 9992.00.00 Accounting Regulations.

## Interpretation


**1** In these Regulations, ***goods*** means goods of tariff item No. 9971.00.00 or 9992.00.00 in the List of Tariff Provisions set out in the schedule to the [Customs Tariff](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md).




## Accounting for Goods


**2** An importer or owner of goods, or any person authorized to account for goods under paragraph 32(6)(a) or under subsection 32(7) of the [Customs Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%201%20(2nd%20Supp.).md), shall, when accounting for the goods under section 32 of that Act, submit
- **(a)** an invoice or written statement from the person who performed the repair or alteration, setting out a detailed description of and the value of the repair or alteration; and
- **(b)** proof of exportation of the goods to the United States, Mexico, Chile, Costa Rica or Israel or another CIFTA beneficiary.
> SOR/2004-127, s. 1.
