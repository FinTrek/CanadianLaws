# Order Respecting the Remission of Customs Duty on Beer Originating in the United States

**SOR/93-418**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CUSTOMS TARIFF](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md)

Date registered: 5 August 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 101 of the [Customs Tariff](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-93-418_e_hq_6260'>[*]</a></sup>, is pleased hereby to make the annexed Order respecting the remission of customs duty on beer originating in the United States.

<a name='fn_SOR-93-418_e_hq_6260'><sup>*</sup></a>: R.S., c. 41 (3rd Supp.)<br />




## Short Title


**1** This Order may be cited as the Beer Originating in the United States Remission Order, 1993.




## Remission


**2** Subject to section 3, remission is hereby granted of the customs duty paid or payable on beer originating in the United States classified under tariff item 2203.00.00 of Schedule I to the [Customs Tariff](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md).




## Conditions


**3** The remission is granted pursuant to section 2 on the condition that:
- **(a)** the beer was released into Canada on or after August 5, 1993; and
- **(b)** a claim for remission is made to the Minister of National Revenue within three years of the date of importation of the beer for which the remission is claimed.
