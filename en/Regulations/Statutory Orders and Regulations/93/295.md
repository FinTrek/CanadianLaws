# Regulations Increasing the Maximum Amounts that Apply in Respect of Appeals under the Informal Procedure

**SOR/93-295**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TAX COURT OF CANADA ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/T/T-2.md)

Date registered: 8 June 1993

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Justice, pursuant to section 18.27<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-93-295_e_hq_6257'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Tax Court of Canada Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/T/T-2.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations increasing the maximum amounts that apply in respect of appeals under the informal procedure.

<a name='fn_SOR-93-295_e_hq_6257'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1990, c. 45, s. 59<br />




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Maximum Amounts for Informal Procedure Regulations.




## Interpretation


**2** In these Regulations, ***Act*** means the [Tax Court of Canada Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/T/T-2.md). (*Loi*)




## Maximum Amounts


**3** The amount of $7,000 referred to in paragraph 18(1)(a), section 18.1, subsections 18.11(2) and (3) and sections 18.12 and 18.13 of the Act is increased to $12,000.



**4** The amount of $14,000 referred to in paragraph 18(1)(b) and sections 18.1, 18.12 and 18.13 of the Act is increased to $24,000.




## Application


**5** Sections 3 and 4 apply in respect of appeals instituted on or after September 1, 1993.
