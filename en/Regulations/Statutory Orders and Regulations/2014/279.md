# Order Declaring that the Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations Do Not Apply in Yukon

**SOR/2014-279**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FISHERIES ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-14.md)

Date registered: 28 November 2014

----------

Whereas the Minister of the Environment has caused to be published in the Canada Gazette, Part I, on June 28, 2014, a copy of the proposed Order Declaring that the Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations Do Not Apply in Yukon, substantially in the annexed form, and persons were given an opportunity to file comments with respect to the proposed order;

And whereas on October 29, 2014, the Minister of the Environment and the Government of Yukon entered into an equivalency agreement under section 4.1<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13359'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Fisheries Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-14.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13369'>[b]</a></sup> to the effect that there are in force by or under the laws of Yukon provisions in the Waters Act<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13360'>[c]</a></sup> and in the Waters Regulation<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13361'>[d]</a></sup> that are equivalent in effect to provisions in the [Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2012/139.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13362'>[e]</a></sup>;

<a name='fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13359'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2012, c. 19, s. 134<br />

<a name='fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13369'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. F-14<br />

<a name='fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13360'><sup>c</sup></a>: S.Y. 2003, c. 19; 2007, c. 6<br />

<a name='fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13361'><sup>d</sup></a>: Y.O.I.C. 2003/58<br />

<a name='fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13362'><sup>e</sup></a>: SOR/2012-139<br />

Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of the Environment, pursuant to subsection 4.2(1)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13359'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Fisheries Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-14.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-1955-E_hq_13369'>[b]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Order Declaring that the Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations Do Not Apply in Yukon.




## Declaration



**Definitions**

**1** For the purposes of sections 2 and 3, ***effluent***, ***final discharge point*** and ***wastewater system*** have the same meanings as in section 1 of the [Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2012/139.md).




**Non-application — Regulations**

**2** The [Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2012/139.md), made under subsection 36(5) of the [Fisheries Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-14.md), do not apply in respect of a wastewater system in Yukon that would otherwise have been subject to those regulations and that is subject to the Waters Act, S.Y. 2003, c. 19; 2007, c. 6 and the Waters Regulation, Yukon O.I.C. 2003/58.




**Non-application — subsection 36(3) of the Act**

**3** Subsection 36(3) of the [Fisheries Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-14.md) does not apply in respect of any deposit of effluent from the final discharge point of a wastewater system referred to in section 2 if the effluent would otherwise have been regulated under the [Wastewater Systems Effluent Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2012/139.md).




## Coming into Force



**Registration**

**4** This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
