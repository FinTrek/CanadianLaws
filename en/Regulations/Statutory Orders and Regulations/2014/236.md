# Special Economic Measures (South Sudan) Permit Authorization Order

**SOR/2014-236**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SPECIAL ECONOMIC MEASURES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1992/c.%2017.md)

Date registered: 24 October 2014

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to subsection 4(4) of the [Special Economic Measures Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1992/c.%2017.md)<sup><a href='#BNP_a'>[a]</a></sup>, authorizes the Minister of Foreign Affairs to issue to any person in Canada or any Canadian outside Canada a permit to carry out a specified activity or transaction, or any class of activity or transaction, that is restricted or prohibited under the [Special Economic Measures (South Sudan) Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/2014/235.md).

<a name='BNP_a'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1992, c. 17<br />
