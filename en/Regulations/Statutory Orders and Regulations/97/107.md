# General Export Permit No. 12 — United States Origin Goods

**SOR/97-107**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXPORT AND IMPORT PERMITS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-19.md)

Date registered: 29 January 1997

----------

The Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to subsections 7(1.1)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup> and 10(1)<sup><a href='#footnoteb_e'>[b]</a></sup> of the [Export and Import Permits Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-19.md), hereby issues the annexed General Export Permit No. 12 — United States Origin Goods.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1994, c. 47, s. 107<br />

<a name='footnoteb_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1994, c. 47, s. 111<br />

Ottawa, January 24, 1997

LLOYD AXWORTHY

*Minister of Foreign Affairs*




## General


**1** Subject to section 2, any person may, under the authority of this Permit, export from Canada any goods of United States origin as described in item 5400 of Group 5 of the Schedule to the [Export Control List](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/89/202.md).



**2** This Permit does not authorize the exportation of goods described in section 1 to any country listed in the [Area Control List](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/81/543.md) or to any of the following countries:
- **(a)** Cuba;
- **(b)** Democratic People’s Republic of Korea;
- **(c)** Iran; and
- **(d)** Syria.
> SOR/99-203, s. 1; SOR/2005-223, s. 1.




**3** Where any goods exported under the authority of this Permit are required to be reported in the prescribed form under the [Customs Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1985/c.%201%20(2nd%20Supp.).md), the statement “GEP-12” or “LGE-12” shall be inserted in the appropriate field of the report.




## Repeal


**4** General Export Permit No. Ex. 12 — United States Origin Goods<sup><a href='#footnote1_e'>[1]</a></sup> is cancelled.

<a name='footnote1_e'><sup>1</sup></a>: SI/89-120<br />




## Coming into Force


**5** This Permit comes into force on January 29, 1997.
