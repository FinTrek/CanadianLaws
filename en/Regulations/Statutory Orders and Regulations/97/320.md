# Canada-Chile Free Trade Agreement Fruit and Vegetable Aggregate Quantity Limit Order

**SOR/97-320**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CUSTOMS TARIFF](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md)

Date registered: 5 July 1997

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 25.8<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Customs Tariff](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md)<sup><a href='#footnoteb_e'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Canada-Chile Free Trade Agreement Fruit and Vegetable Aggregate Quantity Limit Order.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1997, c. 14, s. 51<br />

<a name='footnoteb_e'><sup>b</sup></a>: R.S., c. 41 (3rd Supp.)<br />




## Tariff Rate Quota


**1** The quantity limit of goods of tariff item No. 0703.10.92 that are entitled to the Chile Tariff is, for the period set out in column 1 of item 1 of the schedule, the applicable quantity limit set out in column 2 of that item.
> SOR/98-28, s. 30.




**2** The aggregate quantity limit of goods of tariff item Nos. 0810.10.11 and 0810.10.92 that are entitled to the Chile Tariff is, for the period set out in column 1 of item 2 of the schedule, the applicable aggregate quantity limit set out in column 2 of that item.
> SOR/98-28, s. 30.





## Coming into Force


<sup><a href='#fn_Ind79F1_hq_16411'>[*]</a></sup>**3** This Order comes into force on the day on which section 51 of the [Canada-Chile Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2014.md), chapter 14 of the Statutes of Canada, 1997, comes into force.

<a name='fn_Ind79F1_hq_16411'><sup>*</sup></a>: [Note: Order in force July 5, 1997, *see* SI/97-86.]<br />
