# Regulations Respecting the Advertising, Sale and Importation of Booster Cushions for Children for Use in Motor Vehicles

**SOR/89-446**

Enabling authorities: 
- [HAZARDOUS PRODUCTS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/H/H-3.md)

Date registered: 21 September 1989

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Consumer and Corporate Affairs, pursuant to section 5<sup><a href='#fn_1e'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Hazardous Products Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/H/H-3.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations respecting the advertising, sale and importation of booster cushions for children for use in motor vehicles.

<a name='fn_1e'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1987, c. 30, s. 1<br />




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Hazardous Products (Booster Cushions) Regulations.




## Interpretation


**2** In these Regulations, ***booster cushion*** means any product included in item 45 of Part II of Schedule I to the [Hazardous Products Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/H/H-3.md). (*coussin d’appoint*)




## Authorization


**3** A booster cushion may be advertised, sold or imported only if it meets the specifications set out in Schedule 5 to the [Motor Vehicle Restraint Systems and Booster Cushions Safety Regulations](/en/Regulations/Statutory%20Orders%20and%20Regulations/98/159.md).
> SOR/99-473, s. 4.
