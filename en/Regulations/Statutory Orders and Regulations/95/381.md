# Regulations Prescribing Agricultural Goods in Respect of any Country for the Purpose of Imposing a Safeguard Surtax, 1995-3

**SOR/95-381**

Enabling authorities: 
- [CUSTOMS TARIFF](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md)

Date registered: 1 August 1995

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 60.01(7)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-95-381_e_hq_6359'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Customs Tariff](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1997/c.%2036.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-95-381_e_hq_6360'>[**]</a></sup>, is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations prescribing agricultural goods in respect of any country for the purpose of imposing a safeguard surtax, 1995-3, effective August 1, 1995.

## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Safeguard Surtax Regulations, 1995-3.
