# Regulations Prescribing the Fees To Be Paid by the Users of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System

**SOR/94-537**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md)

Date registered: 4 August 1994

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Solicitor General of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-94-537_e_hq_6299'>[*]</a></sup> of the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md), is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations prescribing the fees to be paid by the users of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System.

<a name='fn_SOR-94-537_e_hq_6299'><sup>*</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 24, s. 6<br />




## Short Title


**1** These Regulations may be cited as the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System Fees Regulations.




## Fee


**2** The fee to be paid by law enforcement agencies for the use of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System is $33.79 annually per sworn police officer.
