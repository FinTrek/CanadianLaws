# Area Control List

**SOR/81-543**

Enabling authorities: 
- [EXPORT AND IMPORT PERMITS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-19.md)

Date registered: 3 July 1981

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Industry, Trade and Commerce, pursuant to sections 4 and 6 of the [Export and Import Permits Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/E/E-19.md), is pleased hereby to revoke the Area Control List, C.R.C., c. 600 and to establish the annexed Area Control List in substitution therefor.
