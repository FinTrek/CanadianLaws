# Weed Seeds Order, 2005

**SOR/2005-220**

Enabling authorities: 
- [SEEDS ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-8.md)

Date registered: 29 June 2005

----------

The Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food, pursuant to subsection 4(2)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Seeds Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-8.md), hereby makes the annexed Weed Seeds Order, 2005.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: R.S., c. 49 (1st Supp.), s. 4(4)<br />

Ottawa, June 28, 2005


<p>Andrew Mitchell<br />Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food<br /></p>




## Weed Seeds


**1** The seeds of the species of plants set out in the schedule are deemed to be weed seeds for the purpose of establishing grades under the [Seeds Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-8.md).




## Repeal


**2** [Repeal]




## Coming into Force


**3** This Order comes into force on July 1, 2005.
