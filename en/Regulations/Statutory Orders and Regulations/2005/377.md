# Regulations Prohibiting Deployments into the Executive Group

**SOR/2005-377**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 24 November 2005

----------

The Treasury Board, pursuant to subsection 26(1)(a) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Regulations Prohibiting Deployments into the Executive Group.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2003, c. 22, ss. 12 and 13<br />

November 21, 2005


<p>Reginald B. Alcock<br />President of the Treasury Board and Minister responsible for the Canadian Wheat Board<br /></p>




## Deployment into Executive Group


**1** A deployment may not be made from any other occupational group into the executive group of an organization named in Schedule I or IV to the [Financial Administration Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/F/F-11.md).




## Coming into Force


**2** These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 12 of the [Public Service Modernization Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022.md), being chapter 22 of the Statutes of Canada, 2003, comes into force.
