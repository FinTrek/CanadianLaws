# Eskasoni Band Council Elections Order

**SOR/2004-105**

Enabling authorities: 
- [INDIAN ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md)

Date registered: 30 April 2004

----------

Whereas the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development deems it advisable for the good government of the Eskasoni Band that the council of the band, consisting of a chief and councillors, shall, beginning on May 1, 2004, be selected by elections to be held in accordance with the [Indian Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md);

Therefore, the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to subsection 74(1) of the [Indian Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md), hereby makes the annexed Eskasoni Band Council Elections Order.

Gatineau, Quebec, April 30, 2004


<p>Andrew Mitchell<br />Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development<br /></p>




## Elections


**1** The council of the Eskasoni Band, consisting of a chief and councillors, shall, beginning on May 1, 2004, be selected by elections held in accordance with the [Indian Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md).
