# Canada Customs and Revenue Agency Regulations, No. 2

**SOR/2004-69**

Enabling authorities: 
- [PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md)

Date registered: 30 March 2004

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 36(1)(b) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md), hereby makes the annexed Canada Customs and Revenue Agency Regulations, No. 2.




## Block Transfer


**1** Subsections 37.3(1) and (2) of the [Public Service Employment Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/2003/c.%2022,%20ss.%2012,%2013%20.md) apply to the portion of the Public Service known as the Canada Customs and Revenue Agency.




## Cessation of Effect


**2** These Regulations cease to have effect at 00:00:03, April 1, 2004.




## Coming into Force


**3** These Regulations come into force at 00:00:01, April 1, 2004.
