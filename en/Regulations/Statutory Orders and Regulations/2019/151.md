# Women Deliver 2019 Global Conference — Privileges and Immunities Order

**SOR/2019-151**

Enabling authorities: 
- [FOREIGN MISSIONS AND INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2041.md)

Date registered: 22 May 2019

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to paragraph 5(1)(c)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-3891_hq_24846'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Foreign Missions and International Organizations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2041.md)<sup><a href='#fn_81000-2-3891_hq_24847'>[b]</a></sup>, makes the annexed Women Deliver 2019 Global Conference — Privileges and Immunities Order.

<a name='fn_81000-2-3891_hq_24846'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2002, c. 12, s. 3(2)<br />

<a name='fn_81000-2-3891_hq_24847'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 41<br />




## Interpretation



**Definition of Conference**

**1** In this Order, ***Conference*** means the Ministerial portion of the Women Deliver 2019 Global Conference, to be held in Vancouver, British Columbia, from June 3 to 5, 2019.




## Privileges and Immunities



**Representatives of foreign states**

**2** From June 1 to 7, 2019, the representatives of a foreign state who are official delegates to the Conference have, to the extent required for the exercise of their functions in Canada in relation to the Conference, the privileges and immunities set out in paragraphs 11(a) to (f) and section 12, to the extent set out in sections 14 to 16, of Article IV of the Convention on the Privileges and Immunities of the United Nations set out in Schedule III to the [Foreign Missions and International Organizations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2041.md).




## Coming into Force



**Registration**

**3** This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
