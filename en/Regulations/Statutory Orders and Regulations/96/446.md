# Nova BUS Corporation Exemption Order

**SOR/96-446**

Enabling authorities: 
- [MOTOR VEHICLE SAFETY ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1993/c.%2016.md)

Date registered: 17 September 1996

----------

His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Transport, pursuant to paragraph 9(1)(c) of the [Motor Vehicle Safety Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1993/c.%2016.md)<sup><a href='#footnotea_e'>[a]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Nova BUS Corporation Exemption Order, being of the opinion that conformity with certain prescribed standards would impede the development of new kinds of buses.

<a name='footnotea_e'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1993, c. 16<br />




## Exemption


**1** Six hundred and fifty units of Nova LFS low-floor model buses, manufactured by Nova BUS Corporation, are exempt from the requirements in subsections 217(11), (14), (17) and (19) of Schedule IV to the [Motor Vehicle Safety Regulations](/en/Regulations/Consolidated%20Regulations%20of%20Canada/1001-1100/C.R.C.,%20c.%201038.md) that are applicable to push-out windows, for a period of two years beginning on the day on which this Order is registered under the [Statutory Instruments Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/S/S-22.md).




## Coming into Force


**2** This Order comes into force on September 17, 1996.
