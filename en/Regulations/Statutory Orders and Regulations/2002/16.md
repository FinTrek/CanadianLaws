# Radiocommunication Act (Paragraph 9(1)(c)) Exemption Order (National Defence and Security)

**SOR/2002-16**

Enabling authorities: 
- [RADIOCOMMUNICATION ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/R/R-2.md)

Date registered: 13 December 2001

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Industry, pursuant to subsection 3(2)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2002-16_e_hq_5829'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Radiocommunication Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/R/R-2.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2002-16_e_hq_5830'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Radiocommunication Act (Paragraph 9(1)(c)) Exemption Order (National Defence and Security).

<a name='fn_SOR-2002-16_e_hq_5829'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 1989, c. 17, s. 4<br />

<a name='fn_SOR-2002-16_e_hq_5830'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1989, c. 17, s. 2<br />




## Exemption


**1** Subject to section 2, Her Majesty in right of Canada is exempt from the application of paragraph 9(1)(c) of the [Radiocommunication Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/R/R-2.md).




## Conditions


**2** The exemption under section 1 applies only if the encrypted subscription programming signal or encrypted network feed
- **(a)** is decoded for the purposes of national defence or security; and
- **(b)** is not available from a lawful distributor.




## Coming into Force


**3** This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
