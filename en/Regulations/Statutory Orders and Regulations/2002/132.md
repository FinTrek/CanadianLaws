## Entity Member of a Bank’s Group



**Entity member of bank’s group *(Entité membre du groupe d’une banque)***

**1** For the purpose of paragraph 464(2)(d) of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md), the following entities are members of a bank’s group:
- **(a)** a central for which an order has been made under subsection 473(1) of the [Cooperative Credit Associations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2048.md) that controls the bank;
- **(b)** a subsidiary of a central referred to in paragraph (a); and
- **(c)** an entity in which a central referred to in paragraph (a) has a substantial investment.




## Entity Member of a Bank Holding Company’s Group



**Entity member of bank holding company’s group *(Entité membre du groupe d’une société de portefeuille bancaire)***

**2** For the purpose of paragraph 925(2)(d) of the [Bank Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2046.md), the following entities are members of a bank holding company’s group:
- **(a)** a central for which an order has been made under subsection 473(1) of the [Cooperative Credit Associations Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2048.md) that controls the bank holding company;
- **(b)** a subsidiary of a central referred to in paragraph (a); and
- **(c)** an entity in which a central referred to in paragraph (a) has a substantial investment.




## Entity Member of Insurance Company’s Group



**Entity member of insurance company’s group *(Entité membre du groupe d’une société d’assurances)***

- **(b)** a subsidiary of a central referred to in paragraph (a); and
- **(c)** an entity in which a central referred to in paragraph (a) has a substantial investment.
