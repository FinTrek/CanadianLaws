# Dakota Tipi Band Council Elections Order

**SOR/2002-316**

Enabling authorities: 
- [INDIAN ACT](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md)

Date registered: 8 August 2002

----------

Whereas the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development deems it advisable for the good government of the Dakota Tipi band that, after August 8, 2002, the council of the band, consisting of a chief and councillors, shall be selected by elections to be held in accordance with the [Indian Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md);

Therefore, the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to subsection 74(1) of the [Indian Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md), hereby makes the annexed Dakota Tipi Band Council Elections Order.

Gatineau, Quebec, August 7, 2002.

Robert D. Nault

Minister of Indian Affairs

and Northern Development




## Elections


**1** The council of the Dakota Tipi band, consisting of a chief and councillors, shall, after August 8, 2002, be selected by elections to be held in accordance with the [Indian Act](/en/Acts/Revised%20Statutes%20of%20Canada/I/I-5.md).




## Coming into Force


**2** This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
