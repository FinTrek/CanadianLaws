# Foreign Institutions Subject to the Canadian Residency Requirements Regulations (Trust and Loan Companies)

**SOR/2003-186**

Enabling authorities: 
- [TRUST AND LOAN COMPANIES ACT](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)

Date registered: 29 May 2003

----------

Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 531<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2003-186_e_hq_5780'>[a]</a></sup> of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md)<sup><a href='#fn_SOR-2003-186_e_hq_5781'>[b]</a></sup>, hereby makes the annexed Foreign Institutions Subject to the Canadian Residency Requirements Regulations (Trust and Loan Companies).

<a name='fn_SOR-2003-186_e_hq_5780'><sup>a</sup></a>: S.C. 2001, c. 9, s. 569<br />

<a name='fn_SOR-2003-186_e_hq_5781'><sup>b</sup></a>: S.C. 1991, c. 45<br />




## Prescribed Holding Body Corporate


**1** For the purpose of subsection 163(2) of the [Trust and Loan Companies Act](/en/Acts/Statutes%20of%20Canada/1991/c.%2045.md), a holding body corporate of a foreign institution is prescribed if
- **(a)** the holding body corporate is incorporated or formed otherwise than by or under an Act of Parliament or of the legislature of a province; and
- **(b)** the holding body corporate, and entities it controls are, when viewed as a whole, engaged primarily in the business of providing financial services.




## Coming into Force


**2** These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
